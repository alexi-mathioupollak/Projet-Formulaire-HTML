Welcome to FRAJ-Form!

Who are we ?

We are students from the IUT of Montpellier-Sète in Computer Science. Our project is to develop a website that allows users to generate forms in HTML code so that they only have to integrate them into the website. Drag and drop, and it's done.

"Generate Does The Job "

Félix R. 2021

We have put together a generator worthy of the name. Simple to use, responsive, efficient and fast, to receive an HTML code to integrate directly on your website.

To use our FRAJ-Form HTML form generator service, simply go to the "GENERATOR" page. There you will find a mini-tutorial to quickly and simply understand how it works. In principle, you can do it even without a tutorial!
